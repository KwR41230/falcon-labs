## The Performance Edge: Speed That Converts

First and foremost, performance is king in the digital age. Slow websites lose customers—studies show that 53% of mobile users abandon sites that take longer than 3 seconds to load. Next.js excels here with server-side rendering (SSR) and static site generation (SSG), which pre-build pages for lightning-fast delivery. Your site loads instantly, improving user experience and SEO rankings.

Compare that to WooCommerce or WordPress sites, which often rely on heavy plugins and themes that bloat load times. Wix, while user-friendly, generates code that's not optimized for speed. A custom Next.js website can handle high traffic without breaking a sweat, ensuring your e-commerce store or business site scales seamlessly.
